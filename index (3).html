About Livarix AI

Precision Medical AI — Cancer Detection & Reporting Platform

medical_services

Livarix AI

AI Cancer Detection & Reporting · v2.0

Livarix AI is an end-to-end clinical decision support platform for liver and lung cancer detection. Using state-of-the-art deep learning — a SegResNet for 3D liver CT segmentation and a custom 3D CNN for lung nodule classification — it assists radiologists with early cancer detection, LI-RADS/Lung-RADS staging, and automated clinical report generation.

Built by undergraduate students from Knowledge Institute of Technology, Salem, this platform directly addresses UN SDG 3 (Good Health & Well-Being) by making AI-assisted oncology accessible at scale.
